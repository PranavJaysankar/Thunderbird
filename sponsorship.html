Hale Education

We are proud to have Hale Education as one of our esteemed sponsors, supporting students in their journey toward higher education. As the GCC’s longest-serving and premier college admissions consulting firm, Hale Education has helped countless students gain admission to top universities worldwide through ethical guidance, personalized mentorship, and expert application support.

Their commitment to excellence is reflected in their 850+ university acceptances for 2024, including 660+ offers from U.S. universities and 120+ acceptances to Top 30 U.S. institutions. Hale’s counselors, graduates of top global universities, provide holistic mentorship, while their proprietary tech platform offers full transparency to students and parents.

Their dedication to shaping strong academic and extracurricular profiles, coupled with their proprietary tech-driven approach, makes them a leader in university admissions.

F1 Bearings

F1 Bearings is a leading provider of precision-engineered bearings, specializing in high-performance solutions for industries including motorsports. As a valued sponsor, F1 Bearings supports the cutting-edge technology and innovation in competitive racing.

Their bearings are built for speed, durability, and efficiency—designed to perform under the most demanding conditions. Beyond motorsports, F1 Bearings proudly supports the F1 in Schools program, inspiring young innovators globally.

Their sponsorship helps students build, design, and race miniature F1 cars, fostering creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving—driving the future of motorsport and engineering.
