About Team Thunderbird

We are Team Thunderbird from Gems Modern Academy, renowned for fostering creativity and excellence among its students. As a diverse group of six young innovators with unique skill sets and aspirations, we are excited to embark on our journey in this competition. Our goal is to win the National Championship and qualify for the World Finals, where we aim to proudly represent the UAE. Each member of our team brings different strengths, ranging from engineering and design to marketing and strategy, allowing us to collaborate effectively in creating a competitive F1 in Schools car.
